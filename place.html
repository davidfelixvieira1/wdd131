About Cristo Redentor

Cristo Redentor is an iconic statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. It is located at the peak of the 700-metre (2,300 ft) Corcovado mountain in the Tijuca Forest National Park, overlooking the city of Rio de Janeiro.
