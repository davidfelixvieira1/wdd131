Project Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: The Power of AI: An Universal Change

Reason for the Name: The name was chosen to reflect the transformative impact of Artificial Intelligence on a global scale, emphasizing its universal significance and potential for change.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to explore and explain the transformative impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) on various aspects of society, technology, and daily life. It will cover topics such as AI in healthcare, education, transportation, entertainment, ethical considerations, future developments, and the role of AI in solving global challenges.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: How is AI transforming the healthcare industry?

Scenario 2: What ethical considerations are associated with the development and deployment of AI?

Color Schema

The selected color scheme for the website includes:

  • Blue (#004080): Used for headings and accents.
  • Light Blue (#80c1ff): Used for backgrounds and secondary elements.
